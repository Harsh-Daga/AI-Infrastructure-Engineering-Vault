# AI Reliability Engineering

SLOs/error budgets for non-deterministic systems, graceful degradation, chaos/failure injection, and cluster health for GPU/training fleets. A discipline that barely exists yet — your SRE background's killer app. Define what "down" means for an LLM, and a degradation ladder for when the big model fails.

> [!info] Why it matters for an infra engineer
> This is your unfair advantage: real reliability discipline in a field that mostly lacks it. Almost no formal practice exists — you can help define it.
