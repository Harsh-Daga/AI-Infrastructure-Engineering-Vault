# Eval as Infrastructure

Continuous, automated evaluation of non-deterministic systems: offline eval sets, LLM-as-judge, regression testing, online eval — i.e. **CI for quality**. The emerging real moat; it's how a "small prompt tweak" doesn't silently regress prod.

> [!info] Why it matters for an infra engineer
> Eval-as-CI is becoming the moat. Alerting on *quality* regressions (not just availability) is the new reliability frontier.
