# AI Gateway

The multi-model edge: virtual-key auth, per-team budgets, rate limits, provider failover, semantic guardrails, and observability — fronting self-hosted + hosted models. The "service mesh for models." Watch the SPOF/bottleneck and supply-chain risks.

> [!info] Why it matters for an infra engineer
> Almost no serious shop runs one provider. The gateway is the new edge and the everyday control plane of a real AI platform.
