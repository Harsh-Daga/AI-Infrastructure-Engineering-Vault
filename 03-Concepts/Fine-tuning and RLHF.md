# Fine-tuning and RLHF

Post-training methods (SFT, RLHF, DPO) that align a base model. You need to know *what* they are and *how they change the infra bill* (e.g. reasoning behaviors → long decode), **not** how to implement them.

> [!info] Why it matters for an infra engineer
> Know the serving consequences (decode-heavy behavior, adapters/LoRA serving) without sinking time into training internals.
