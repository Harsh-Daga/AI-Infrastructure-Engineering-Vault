# Part 3 — The 52-Week Daily Plan

**Daily structure — every single day (1 hour):**
- **Learn (20m):** highest-quality explainer for the day's concept.
- **Read (15m):** an engineering writeup, blog, or paper section.
- **Build (20m):** hands-on — small daily increments compound into the Part 4 projects.
- **Reflect (5m):** answer the week's question in a running engineering journal (your public learning log).

The plan is split into six phases. Open [[✅ This Week]] each day to see the active week.
